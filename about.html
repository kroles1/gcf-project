Therapy House provides range of therapies to assist you with any issue you are facing. We are determined to make professional therapy convenient and relaxing. Our House was built in calming, yet accessible area just 20 minutes out of the city. Tell us what you need or what is your issue and we will customise our service for you. Our offer includes regular sessions (group or individual) and one-off events. Other than sessions, you can spend your day taking part in relaxing activities. We also have an eco-friendly, sustainable farm located on Theraphy House's grounds. You can also connect your vacation with wellbeing by choosing a longer stay (up to three weeks).
